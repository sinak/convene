Frequently Asked Questions

How does it work?

You list up your product or service, decide how far you want to deliver, and set a price. You can bring your own customers on board or wait for Convene customers to order from you.

How much does it cost?

Convene is in beta. Right now we're only charging a nominal 2.5% fee to cover credit card charges.

What happens if I'm busy?

You can mark yourself as "available" in Convene and your listing will be live. If you're not available, just mark yourself as "busy" and users won't be able to order.

How will my service be listed?

Your product or service will be listed among other Convene services available in the customer's neighborhood.
